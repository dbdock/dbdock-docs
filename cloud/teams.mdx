A team lets more than one person work in the same DBDock workspace. Members manage the same connections, backups, storage, and schedules — no sharing of a single login required.

## How it works

The workspace has an **owner** and **members**. Members act on the owner's resources: they see the same connections and backups, and their actions run against the owner's account and plan. This keeps billing and quotas in one place while giving your team shared access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Team page">
    You'll see your workspace, current members, and available seats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite a member">
    Send an invite by email. The recipient accepts, signs in, and joins your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage access">
    Remove members or re-invite as your team changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plan availability

Team members are a paid feature, with a per-plan seat limit. If your plan doesn't include team seats yet, you'll be prompted to upgrade when you try to invite someone. See [Billing](/cloud/billing).

<Info>
  Because members act on the owner's resources, everyone shares the workspace's managed-storage quota and job limits. Plan seats and quotas together — see the [pricing page](https://dbdock.xyz/pricing).
</Info>
